AHMED HASEN

I am a Doctoral Researcher at AI-DOC, University of Jyvaskyla (JYU) (2024-2027), working on deep learning methods for motion tracking and analysis. Before starting my doctoral studies, I completed my Master's (2021-2023) and Bachelor's (2017-2021) in Software and Information Engineering at UESTC, Chengdu, China.

My research focuses on deep learning, computer vision, 3D reconstruction, object tracking, and motion analysis in sports. I am also interested in agentic AI and practical AI engineering for real-world deployment. Open to collaborate, reach out if you are interested.
